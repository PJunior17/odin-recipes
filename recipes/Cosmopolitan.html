Cosmopolitan

Description

I know who you are. You are someone who is looking to party and while drinking something super tasty. Well I've got the drink for you, but BEWARE this is too easy to drink if you're not careful.

Ingredients

  • Aboslute Citron
  • Cointreau
  • Cranberry Juice
  • Simple Syrup
  • Seltzer Water
  • Ice
  • Lime Juice

Steps

  1. 1 OZ of lime juice in a shaker
  2. 1/2 OZ of simple syrup
  3. 2-4 OZ of cranberry juice, amount depends on your preference
  4. 2 OZ of Absolute Citron
  5. 1 OZ of cointreau
  6. Some Ice
  7. Shake all those ingredients until chilled and mixed
  8. Pour into a glass
  9. Pour 3-6 OZ of seltzer water in your cup
  10. Garnish with a lime or lemon wheel and you're ready to serve!
