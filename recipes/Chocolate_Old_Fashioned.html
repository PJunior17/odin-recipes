Chocolate Old Fashioned

Description

If you're looking for an after dinner drink that you could really spend time sipping and appreciating the spirit this is definetly the drink you!

Ingredients

  • Japanese Akashi Whiskey
  • Chocolate Bitters
  • Sugar or Simple Syrup
  • Ice

Steps

  1. If you are using a sugar cube, granulated sugar then place your bitters in and muddle. If you are using simple syrup then just put about a bar spoon of it in with your bitters.
  2. Put your ice in and 2 OZ of the Akashi Whiskey
  3. Stir cocktail until sugars are mixed in and you should be ready to enjoy
  4. You could garnish this with an orange peel or you could do no garnish at all!
